Summary:Lattitude vs Maximum Temperature

This is a plot cross referencing the distance from the equator equator and the maximum temperature recorded in the nearest city to a randomly generated lattitude and longitude. It is apparaent that at the equator the maximum temperature is greatest and as we move in either direction(north or south) from the equtor the maximum temperature begins to decrease. Be aware that the graph is not completely parabolic as there is greter land mass above(+lat)the equator than below (-lat)
